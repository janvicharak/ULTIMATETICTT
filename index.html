Game Rules

  • Game has 9 small tic-tac-toe boards forming a larger board.
  • Win a small board by getting 3 of your marks in a row.
  • Your move determines where the opponent must play next.
  • If directed board is won or full, opponent may play anywhere.
  • Win the game by winning 3 small boards in a row on the large board.
